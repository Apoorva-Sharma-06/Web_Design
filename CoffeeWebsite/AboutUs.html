About Us

Our voyage from the bean to the container began in year 2000. Bistro had not set its impression and espresso was as yet thought about an extravagance. Our mission to give a genuinely global affair and mix a really Italian cuppa, drove us to make the brand Coffee Bar . Leading the Indian bistro culture, Coffee Bar was a chance to find both the espresso and the art. Nobody has aced espresso superior to the Italians. Tipping our caps their direction, Coffee Bar conveys new significance to Italian espresso and eats in the Indian subcontinent. Today, we keep on blending espresso the manner in which world does and in our adventure we have made espresso associations with our clients crosswise over India, USA, Sri Lanka, Maldives, Bangladesh, Nepal and Myanmar.
